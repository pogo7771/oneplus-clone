About OnePlus

OnePlus Technology Co., Ltd., commonly referred to as OnePlus, is a Chinese smartphone manufacturer owned by BBK Electronics, based in Shenzhen, Guangdong. Founded by Pete Lau and Carl Pei in December 2013, the company is renowned for its premium smartphones.

From the OnePlus One in 2014 to the latest flagships, OnePlus continues to push the boundaries of technology. With a focus on speed, design, and user experience, we strive to deliver the best Android experience possible.
