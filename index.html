About OnePlus

One Plus Technology Co., Ltd., commonly referred to as OnePlus, is a Chinese smartphone manufacturer owned by BBK Electronics, which is based in Shenzhen, Guangdong. It was founded by Pete Lau and Carl Pei in December 2013. Primary industry refers to the industry from which a company drives its maximum revenue.

OnePlus was founded by Pete Lau and Carl Pei on 16 December 2013 to develop a high-end flagship smartphone running Cyanogen OS that would come to be known as the OnePlus One. OnePlus would continue to release phones throughout the 2010s and 2020s. In 2020, OnePlus released the OnePlus Nord.
